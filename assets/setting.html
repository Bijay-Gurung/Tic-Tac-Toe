Purpose: for players to place their 3 allotted marks (X or O) in a continuous line, vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. Rules: each player chooses a mark and takes turns to place them on the grid. The game is over when a player gets a continuous line of 3, or the 9-square grid is full. How to play: players take turns to place their marks in an attempt to get a continuous line of 3 cells. Strategy: it is a solved game, meaning that there are strategies that can be used for the best result in each game.
